INDIAN DISHES

Litti Chokha
One of the best dish an indian can have is Litti Chokha. It is one of the popular street food of Bihar. Litti is made up of flour and sattu whereas chokha is made by brinjal, potato and some other ingredients to enhance its taste.
Biryani
Thinking about Biryani makes our mouth full of sliva. One of the popular food of Hydrabad. Biryani is all the way rice, only way changes is the way of cooking and ingredients added to it.
Panipuri
This street food is loved by every single indian. It is famous in almost all the parts of india.
